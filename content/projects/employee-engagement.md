# Employee Engagement Platform

A conceptual SaaS platform designed to improve employee engagement using gamification, feedback loops, and AI-driven insights.

---

## Overview

The platform focuses on building continuous engagement between employees and organisations through:

- Recognition systems
- Feedback loops
- Rewards and gamification
- Analytics dashboards
- AI-based sentiment insights

---

## Architecture Highlights

- Multi-tenant SaaS architecture
- Event-driven engagement tracking
- Scalable microservice design
- AI-based engagement scoring

---

## Key Capabilities

- Employee recognition engine
- Real-time engagement analytics
- Survey & feedback system
- Manager dashboards
- AI-driven insights
