# AI Product Information Platform

A system designed to centralize product data and enhance it using AI-driven enrichment pipelines.

---

## Overview

The platform enables:

- Product aggregation from multiple sources
- AI-based enrichment
- Multi-language translation
- Image enhancement
- Structured catalog management

---

## Key Features

- Unified product database
- AI-generated descriptions
- Automated tagging & categorization
- Search optimization
- API-first architecture
